Delicious Milkshake

Description

A yummy, gob-smackingly drinkable milkshake. Filled with all that good stuff that people's mouths like, such as, milks and shakes. And cinnamon on top. It can even be put into a glass.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 cup Chila Horchata
  • 1/4 cup espresso
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Top with whipped cream and sprinkle with cinnamon.
